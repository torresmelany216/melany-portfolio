Hi, I'm Melany!

I've been passionate about content creation since I was 10 years old, long before I even knew what a "content creator" was. I'd pick up any camera I could find and just start talking. Over time, my phone stopped being a device and started feeling like my best friend.

I'm a lifestyle content creator and a proud Latina from Chicago. Representing my culture is everything to me, whether that's bringing my parents on camera, sharing our food, or just existing as myself every day. (My audience might actually love my mom more than they love me, and honestly? Same.)

My goal is to turn this passion into a full-time career. And I'm already on my way.
